About Us

At KathaLens, we believe every moment tells a story. Based in Columbus, Ohio, our mission is to capture the essence of life through the lens, turning fleeting moments into lasting memories. The name "Katha" means "story" in Sanskrit, and that’s exactly what we aim to craft with every shot — a visual narrative that speaks to the heart. Whether it's a wedding, a portrait, or an event, we're here to tell your unique story, one frame at a time. We specialize in portraits, including senior and graduation pictures, as well as event and real estate photography. Let us help you preserve your story!
